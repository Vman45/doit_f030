8.4.1 ScreenEye + HandyCam


How to conncect your ScreenEye or Expose' to a HandyCam

Schematics to connect your Handycam to your Falcon ScreenEye.
Use only single shilded cables to wire all connections.



Abbildung 1 - Video- + Audioverkabelung



I fix my F030 with a female chinch plug at the backside to support the internal screeneye.
So i can use a selfmade cable to transfer video and audio to F030. After wirering you have to start SND_THRU.PRG in autofolder or seperate. Start ScreenEye- Software or APEX and you got live pictures with sound on your F030.

With mono-handycam you only need one soundwire.
Do not use ANY cable. Use cable with seperate shilded audio and video wires for the best signal quality!
